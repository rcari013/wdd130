MANGALDAN

CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

We believe in local businesses.

We believe that creating a network for other business leaders and community members will better our local economy and improve the quality of life. Business leaders and chamber members can work together to get things done in our community – as a collective voice.

The Mangaldan Chamber of Commerce isn’t just for the small mom-and-pop stores, but for the local banks, franchises, major retailers, and local sports teams. There are no limit or conditions to join the chamber. Anyone or any business that wants to join to better the community is invited to join us.

Chamber Objectives

  • Help Businesses Grow
  • Increase Job Opportunities
  • Contribute to the overall economic stability of the community
